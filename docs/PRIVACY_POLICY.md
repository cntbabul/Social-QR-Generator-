# Privacy Policy for Social QR

**Last updated:** February 16, 2026

Refer to this Privacy Policy to understand how Social QR ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. No Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, share, or sell any of your personal data.**

All data processing, including QR code generation and scanning, happens locally on your device. We do not transmit your information to any external servers.

## 2. Permissions and Usage

To provide the App's functionality, we request access to the following permissions solely for the purposes listed below:

### Camera Access
We request access to your device's camera **strictly for the purpose of scanning QR codes**. We do not record, save, or transmit any images or video feeds from your camera.

### Gallery and Storage Access
We request access to your device's photo gallery and storage **strictly to allow you to save the QR codes** you generate to your device. We do not access, view, or upload any other photos or files from your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect, track, or analyze user data.

## 4. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy.
